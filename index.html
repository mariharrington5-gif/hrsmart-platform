🆕 New
CA SB 988 — Freelance Worker Protection Act Extension
Effective July 1, 2026 · California · Affects: Independent Contractor Policy
▼
What Changed
California extended protections for freelance/contract workers. Companies hiring independent contractors for $250+ must provide a written contract. Contractors now have a private right of action for violations, with potential penalties of $1,000 per contract plus attorney's fees.
Affected Handbook Section
Section 3 — Classification of Workers / Independent Contractors
Language must be updated to reflect written contract requirements and contractor rights.
HR Action Required
Review all active contractor arrangements. Ensure written contracts are in place. Update handbook policy language and IC onboarding checklist.
🆕 New
Federal DOL — Overtime Rule Update (Salary Threshold Increase)
Effective January 1, 2026 · Federal · Affects: Compensation Policy, FLSA Classification
▼
What Changed
The Department of Labor raised the minimum salary threshold for overtime exemption from $684/week ($35,568/year) to $1,128/week ($58,656/year). Employees below this threshold must receive overtime pay regardless of their job duties or title.
Affected Handbook Section
Section 7 — Compensation & Pay Practices
Exempt/non-exempt classification language and overtime policy must reflect the new threshold.
HR Action Required
Audit all exempt employee salaries against new threshold. Reclassify any employees below $58,656/yr as non-exempt. Update handbook compensation section.
🆕 New
CA AB 2123 — Expanded Paid Sick Leave for New Parents
Effective January 1, 2026 · California · Affects: Leave Policy, Sick Leave Section
▼
What Changed
California now requires employers to provide an additional 5 days of paid sick leave specifically for use in the first 12 months following the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child. This is in addition to existing CFRA/FMLA entitlements and accrued sick leave balances.
Affected Handbook Section
Section 5 — Leave of Absence Policies / Sick Leave
New parent sick leave entitlement must be added. Baby bonding policy cross-reference should be updated.
HR Action Required
Update sick leave policy section. Notify payroll to track the new leave category. Send communication to all employees who had a qualifying event in the past 12 months.
✅ Approved
